LYFESTYLE

Lyfestyle is a very recent album made by the well-known hip-hop artist Yeat. In this album, he was able to capture many hearts by recreating the feel of all of his unique sounds, way back from his 2021 sound to his new, futuristic and cinematic sound in his second latest album 2093. He was also able to capture his high pitched sound, making the album extremely stylised and unique to him, therefore he has a dedicated fanbase that supports what he does.

2093

2093 was the album previously released before Lyfestyle, by rapper Noah Smith going under the moniker Yeat. In this album, he exprerimented with different sounds and gave it an intense futuristic and cinematic touch. Most fans were not pleased with this album, as it was particularly different to his ohers.
